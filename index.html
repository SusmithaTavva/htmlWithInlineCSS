Ingredients:

  • Oregano
  • Olive Oil
  • Crushed Red Pepper
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon yeast
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 475¬∞F (245¬∞C).
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, salt, and yeast. Add warm water and knead into a dough.
  3. Roll out the dough into a circle and place it on a baking sheet.

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 3 cups flour

ü•ó Caesar Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard
  • 1 head romaine lettuce
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 1/2 cup Caesar dressing
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Wash and chop the romaine lettuce into bite-sized pieces.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, mustard, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place the lettuce in a large salad bowl.
  4. Drizzle the dressing over the lettuce and toss to coat evenly.
  5. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese and croutons over the salad.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!

üçù Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 teaspoon chili flakes
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 eggs
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • 100g pancetta
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic

Instructions:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the spaghetti until al dente.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and cook the pancetta until crispy. Add minced garlic and chili flakes.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs and grated Parmesan cheese.
  4. Drain the pasta, reserving some pasta water, and add it to the pan with pancetta.
  5. Remove the pan from heat and quickly mix in the egg and cheese mixture, stirring constantly.
  6. Add a little reserved pasta water to achieve a creamy consistency.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve immediately with extra Parmesan on top.

ü•û Fluffy Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup fresh blueberries
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, beat the egg and mix in milk, vanilla extract, and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients, stirring until just mixed.
  4. Heat a lightly greased pan over medium heat.
  5. Pour batter onto the pan and cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip.
  6. Cook until golden brown and remove from heat.
  7. Serve warm with fresh blueberries and syrup.
